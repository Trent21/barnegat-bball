Head Varsity Coach:
Michael Puorro.

Coach Puorro is entering his 14th year of coaching high school basketball and will be entering his 4th season as the Head Basketball Coach at Barnegat High School. Prior to his arrival at Barnegat, Coach Puorro spent two seasons as the Assistant Varsity Coach at Point Pleasant Borough, two seasons as the Freshman Basketball Coach at Barnegat High School and six seasons as the Head Basketball Coach at Wall Township HS. Coach Puorro possesses a great deal of knowledge regarding what it takes to be successful in the game of basketball as he was the Boy's All-Time Leading Scorer at Point Pleasant Borough High School from 2002-2012 (Currently is second).

    Here is a list of accolades Coach Puorro has received:

  • 2016-2017 Division Coach of the Year (B-South)
  • 2014-2015 Division Coach of the Year (B-South)
  • 2012-2013 Star Ledger - Monmouth County Coach of the Year
  • 2012-2013 Division Coach of the Year (B-North)
